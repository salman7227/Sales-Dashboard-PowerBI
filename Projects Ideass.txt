Businesses must keep detailed records of their sales for a variety of reasons. Analyzing sales data allows companies to keep track of their financial health and answer all critical questions regarding their performance.

Dataset: Microsoft's sample dataset. <<--(Resource Link)

Project Idea: Create a Power BI dashboard to analyze sales performance and profit distribution across products and regions. Start by importing the company’s sales and financial data into Power BI, then use Power Query to clean and organize fields like product category, transaction date, region, and profit. Build a line chart to track monthly and yearly profit trends, helping spot seasonality and growth patterns over time. Next, visualize profit by country across each product segment using a bar chart, where each bar represents a specific segment within a country. For a global view, add a world map visual to showcase geographic profit distribution. Use clustered column charts to highlight top and bottom performers based on profit margins. Segment products into categories and use treemaps or stacked bar visuals to uncover strong and weak performers. This sales data analysis project will serve as a decision-making companion for sales and strategy teams, revealing high-impact products, regional opportunities, and trends that shape profitability.
